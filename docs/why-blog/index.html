Harishankar

Nov 27, 2021 • 1 min read

Tried a few times before and gave up on blogging each time! Even micro blogging didn't work well for me. After all the failure attempts, this time trying something different to see if I can do it.

Goal

  • Keep a log of the hobby projects for future reference and memories
  • Try something that I always thought I am not good at
  • Assuming writing helps me keep focus on things! Prove it!

  • Post random topics and pictures like this one
